How each SKU's probability is calculated

Here SKUs (PLG OH > Syrup IA): inventory is on hand, so probability is 100% — Confirmed. Both "Here - Allocate This Week" and "Here - Allocate Next Week" SKUs land here.

Pending SKUs (waiting on WIP): probability is keyed off the buffer days between the WIP-pending date and the alloc drop date — more buffer means higher confidence. Pending SKUs always carry some WIP risk and are capped at 97%, so they never reach the Confirmed bucket.

  • 95% 3+ days early
  • 90% 2 days early
  • 85% 1 day early
  • 75% same day
  • 55% 1 day late
  • 40% 2 days late
  • 25% 3+ days late

Modifiers stacked on top: SKUs with PLG already partially staged get the on-hand portion guaranteed (1.0); on-time history adds ±5%; a generosity multiplier (default 1.05×, adjustable in Settings) leans estimates slightly high to keep the warehouse from being underprepared.

Bucket meanings: Confirmed (≥99%, on-hand only) · Likely (80–98%) · Possible (50–79%) · Long-shot (<50%).

Roll-forward: a Pending SKU at p% this week contributes (1−p)% to next week — the remaining probability has to drop somewhere.

Excluded: Pending SKUs without a WIP date (no signal to estimate from). They appear in the Risk Factors strip below.

Bulk Update SKUs
Apply a note, a CS tier override, a PS tier override, or any combination to many SKUs at once. Empty fields are skipped.

Status outcomes by note prefix:
• ADJ (other adjustment) → blanket Adjusted regardless of count. Trusted reason, no math.
• ISS+{N} / ISS-{N} (retroactive) → describes a Syrup change that already happened. If the encoded count matches the actual matrix↔configs gap, status is Correct. Otherwise Off.
• SR{N} (forward-looking) → declares a planned store removal. Flips status to Off so the SKU surfaces in Generate Configs. Once you actually update Syrup and the gap matches, the SKU returns to Correct.
• ADD{N} (forward-looking) → declares a planned store addition — opposite of SR. Flips status to Off until configs catch up. Use when adding a store to a SKU outside its tier.
• DROP (dropped from assortment) → SKU was killed upstream (production, costing, etc) before reaching stores. Status flips to Dropped. Both ct and dt checks are suppressed and the SKU is excluded from the Configs Generator entirely.
• MD (markdown exit) → SKU is exiting via markdown. Status flips to Markdown when configured=0 (clean) or ⚠ Markdown! when Syrup still has stores configured (zero them out). Excluded from the Configs Generator.
• SR/ISS without an encoded count (legacy notes) → can't verify, status stays Off. Re-enter with a count to clear.

Set PS tier to None to force a SKU to Newness (matches Excel's CJ4="None" override). All overrides immediately recompute Config Status, store count, depth, and downstream fields.
